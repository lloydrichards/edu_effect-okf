# Monstera Yellow Leaves

Monstera yellow leaves over months usually indicate a chronic condition rather than a one-time shock. The most useful checks are root-zone moisture, light level, pest inspection, and whether yellowing is limited to old leaves.

## Focused Routes

* Wet or sour mix: [yellow leaves from overwatering](/problems/yellow-leaves-overwatering.md).
* Slow growth, long internodes, or a distant window: [yellow leaves from low light](/problems/yellow-leaves-low-light.md).
* Stippling, residue, webbing, or insects: [yellow leaves from pests](/problems/yellow-leaves-pests.md).
* Mushy roots or wet-soil wilting: [root rot](/problems/root-rot.md).

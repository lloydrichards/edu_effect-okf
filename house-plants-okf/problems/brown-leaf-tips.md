# Brown Leaf Tips

Brown leaf tips can arise from several interacting stresses rather than a single universal cause.

## Common Causes

* Low [relative humidity](/care/relative-humidity.md)
* [Soluble salts](/care/soluble-salts.md) or excess [fertilizer](/care/fertilizer.md)
* [Underwatering](/care/underwatering.md)
* Root stress from [overwatering](/care/overwatering.md)
* Sensitive plants such as prayer plants, dracaenas, spider plants, and ferns

Correct the likely cause, then judge success by new growth rather than old damaged tips.

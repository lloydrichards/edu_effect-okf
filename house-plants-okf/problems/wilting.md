# Wilting

Wilting means the plant cannot maintain water pressure in tissues. The key diagnostic split is whether the potting medium is dry or wet.

## Dry Soil

Dry soil suggests [underwatering](/care/underwatering.md), a too-small pot, hydrophobic mix, or high water demand.

## Wet Soil

Wet soil suggests [overwatering](/care/overwatering.md), poor [drainage hole](/care/drainage-hole.md) function, dense [growing medium](/care/growing-medium.md), or [root rot](/problems/root-rot.md).

Thin-leaved foliage plants often wilt visibly when dry, while succulents may show subtler signs.

# Leaf Scorch

Leaf scorch occurs when foliage receives more light or heat than it can tolerate, especially after sudden movement from shade to [direct sun](/care/direct-sun.md).

## Remedies

* Move sensitive plants to [bright indirect light](/care/bright-indirect-light.md).
* Use gradual [acclimation](/foundations/acclimation.md).
* Recheck [window exposure](/care/window-exposure.md), especially south and west windows.

Shade-adapted foliage plants are more vulnerable than high-light succulents.

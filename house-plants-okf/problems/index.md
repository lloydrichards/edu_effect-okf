# Problems

Diagnostic symptoms and disorders that connect care conditions to plant outcomes.

* [Leggy Growth](./leggy-growth.md) - Stretching caused mainly by insufficient light.
* [Leaf Scorch](./leaf-scorch.md) - Bleached or burned tissue from excess light or abrupt exposure.
* [Yellowing Leaves](./yellowing-leaves.md) - Non-specific symptom requiring context.
* [Yellow Leaves From Overwatering](./yellow-leaves-overwatering.md) - Yellowing with wet soil or root oxygen stress.
* [Yellow Leaves From Low Light](./yellow-leaves-low-light.md) - Yellowing with weak growth and slow drying.
* [Yellow Leaves From Pests](./yellow-leaves-pests.md) - Yellowing with sap-feeding pest evidence.
* [Monstera Yellow Leaves](./monstera-yellow-leaves.md) - Plant-specific yellowing routes for Monstera.
* [Diagnostic Triage](./diagnostic-triage.md) - First-pass routing from symptom to focused cause.
* [Brown Leaf Tips](./brown-leaf-tips.md) - Leaf-edge damage from humidity, salts, water, or roots.
* [Wilting](./wilting.md) - Loss of turgor from water deficit or root failure.
* [Leaf Drop](./leaf-drop.md) - Stress response after environmental or root disruption.
* [Root Rot](./root-rot.md) - Root decay from wet, poorly aerated conditions.

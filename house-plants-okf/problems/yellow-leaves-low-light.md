# Yellow Leaves From Low Light

Yellow leaves from low light are likely when new growth is weak, internodes stretch, the plant leans toward a window, or the pot stays wet longer than expected.

## Check

Assess [insufficient light](/care/insufficient-light.md), then change placement gradually. If leaves are also burned or bleached, consider [light acclimation](/care/light-acclimation.md) and [leaf scorch](/problems/leaf-scorch.md).

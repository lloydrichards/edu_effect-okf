# Leggy Growth

Leggy growth shows as long internodes, sparse foliage, small leaves, weak stems, or vines reaching toward a window.

## Likely Cause

The common cause is inadequate [light intensity](/care/light-intensity.md), especially when a plant is too far from a window or a grow light is too weak.

## Remedies

* Move toward better [window exposure](/care/window-exposure.md).
* Add [artificial lighting](/care/artificial-lighting.md).
* Prune and propagate with [stem cuttings](/propagation/stem-cuttings.md) after light improves.

Vining and climbing plants often show this symptom clearly because internode spacing changes visibly.

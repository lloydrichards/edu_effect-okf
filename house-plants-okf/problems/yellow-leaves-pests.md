# Yellow Leaves From Pests

Yellow leaves from pests are likely when yellowing appears with stippling, sticky residue, webbing, cottony clusters, or scale-like bumps.

## Check

Inspect undersides, petioles, nodes, and leaf axils. If pests are present, start with [quarantine](/pests/quarantine.md) and follow [pest response workflow](/workflows/pest-response-workflow.md).

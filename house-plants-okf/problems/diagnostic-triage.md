# Diagnostic Triage

Use diagnostic triage when the symptom is clear but the cause is not.

1. Check current root-zone moisture with [soil moisture check](/care/soil-moisture-check.md).
2. If soil is wet, consider [yellow leaves from overwatering](/problems/yellow-leaves-overwatering.md) or [root rot](/problems/root-rot.md).
3. If growth is stretched or slow, consider [yellow leaves from low light](/problems/yellow-leaves-low-light.md).
4. If there is webbing, sticky residue, or visible insects, consider [yellow leaves from pests](/problems/yellow-leaves-pests.md).

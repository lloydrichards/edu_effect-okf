# Yellow Leaves From Overwatering

Yellow leaves from overwatering are most likely when the pot stays wet, smells sour, drains poorly, or wilts while the mix is still moist.

## Check

Use a [soil moisture check](/care/soil-moisture-check.md). If roots are damaged, compare with [root rot](/problems/root-rot.md). Correct the cause by improving drainage, medium structure, and dry-down timing.

# Yellowing Leaves

Yellowing leaves are non-specific. Good diagnosis depends on pattern, location, soil moisture, recent changes, and pest evidence.

## Focused Diagnostic Routes

* [Diagnostic triage](/problems/diagnostic-triage.md) for first-pass routing.
* [Yellow leaves from overwatering](/problems/yellow-leaves-overwatering.md) when soil stays wet.
* [Yellow leaves from low light](/problems/yellow-leaves-low-light.md) when growth is weak or stretched.
* [Yellow leaves from pests](/problems/yellow-leaves-pests.md) when leaves show stippling, webbing, residue, or insects.

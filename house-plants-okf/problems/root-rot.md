# Root Rot

Root rot is a root-zone failure where roots become brown or black, mushy, and unable to supply water even when the pot is wet.

## Causes

* Chronic [overwatering](/care/overwatering.md)
* Missing or blocked [drainage hole](/care/drainage-hole.md)
* Dense or degraded [growing medium](/care/growing-medium.md)
* Low [light intensity](/care/light-intensity.md) reducing water use

## Symptoms and Remedies

Symptoms include wet-soil [wilting](/problems/wilting.md), [yellowing leaves](/problems/yellowing-leaves.md), and [leaf drop](/problems/leaf-drop.md). Remedies include removing rotten roots, [repotting](/care/repotting.md) in fresh draining medium, and changing [watering](/care/watering.md).

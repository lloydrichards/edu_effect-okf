# Houseplant Source Anchors

This bundle uses source-backed general horticultural guidance rather than unsourced social-media plant care rules.

## Core References

* University of Georgia Extension, "Growing Indoor Plants with Success": https://extension.uga.edu/publications/detail.html?number=B1318&title=growing-indoor-plants-with-success
* University of Illinois Extension, "Houseplants": https://extension.illinois.edu/houseplants
* Clemson Cooperative Extension HGIC, "Indoor Plants - Cleaning, Fertilizing, Containers & Light Requirements": https://hgic.clemson.edu/factsheet/indoor-plants-cleaning-fertilizing-containers-light-requirements/
* Royal Horticultural Society, "Houseplants": https://www.rhs.org.uk/plants/types/houseplants
* Royal Horticultural Society, "Houseplant 101": https://www.rhs.org.uk/plants/types/houseplants/houseplant-101
* ASPCA Animal Poison Control, "Toxic and Non-Toxic Plants List": https://www.aspca.org/pet-care/animal-poison-control/toxic-and-non-toxic-plants
* Missouri Botanical Garden Plant Finder: https://www.missouribotanicalgarden.org/plantfinder/plantfindersearch.aspx

Use these references when expanding the bundle. This page intentionally avoids linking to every sourced concept so it remains a bibliographic node rather than a graph super-hub.

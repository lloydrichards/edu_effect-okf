# Stem Cuttings

Stem cuttings use a section of stem, usually with at least one node, to root a new plant. They are common for vining and aroid houseplants.

Cuttings work best when the parent plant has adequate [light intensity](/care/light-intensity.md) and is not stressed by [root rot](/problems/root-rot.md) or pests.

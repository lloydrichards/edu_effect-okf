# Leaf Cuttings

Leaf cuttings are useful for some succulents and plants with regenerative leaf tissue. They are not universal; many aroids need nodes and cannot be propagated from leaf blades alone.

Rot risk rises if cuttings sit in wet [growing medium](/care/growing-medium.md) or receive poor [light intensity](/care/light-intensity.md).

# Division

Division separates an existing plant into multiple rooted pieces. It is often done during [repotting](/care/repotting.md).

After division, protect plants from [underwatering](/care/underwatering.md), [overwatering](/care/overwatering.md), and excessive [direct sun](/care/direct-sun.md) while roots recover.

# Soil Moisture Check

A soil moisture check asks what the root zone is doing right now, not when the plant was last watered. Check the surface, deeper mix, pot weight, drainage behavior, and whether wet soil matches symptoms.

## Diagnostic Use

* Wet mix plus yellowing or wilting points toward [yellow leaves from overwatering](/problems/yellow-leaves-overwatering.md).
* Bone-dry mix plus limp leaves points toward [underwatering](/care/underwatering.md).
* Uneven wet-dry pockets can indicate old or hydrophobic [growing medium](/care/growing-medium.md).

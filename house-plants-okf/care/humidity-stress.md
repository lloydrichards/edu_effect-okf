# Humidity Stress

Humidity stress appears when air is dry enough to increase leaf water loss beyond what roots and tissues can comfortably replace.

## Diagnostic Use

* Tip burn can appear as [brown leaf tips](/problems/brown-leaf-tips.md).
* Dry indoor air can favor [spider mites](/pests/spider-mites.md).
* A local [microclimate](/foundations/microclimate.md) may reduce stress.

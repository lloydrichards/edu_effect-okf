# Growing Medium

Growing medium, often called potting mix, is the root environment. Good mix provides water, oxygen, anchorage, and space for roots without becoming persistently saturated.

Dense or degraded mix contributes to [overwatering](/care/overwatering.md), root decay, and poor [watering](/care/watering.md) control.

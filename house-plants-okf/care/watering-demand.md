# Watering Demand

Watering demand rises or falls with light, temperature, humidity, root health, pot size, and medium. The same plant may need different care in winter, after repotting, or after moving closer to a window.

## Main Drivers

* Stronger light and warmer temperatures usually increase demand.
* Dense or oversized pots may stay wet even if the plant is thirsty above ground.
* Damaged roots can make wet soil behave like drought because the plant cannot absorb water.

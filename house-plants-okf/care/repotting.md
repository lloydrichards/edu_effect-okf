# Repotting

Repotting refreshes [growing medium](/care/growing-medium.md), corrects container problems, and gives roots more room when needed. It is not automatically beneficial; oversized pots can worsen [overwatering](/care/overwatering.md).

## When to Repot

* Roots circle the pot or exit the [drainage hole](/care/drainage-hole.md).
* Water runs through without wetting old mix.
* Medium has collapsed, smells sour, or supports root decay.
* Plant is unstable or severely pot-bound.

See [repotting workflow](/workflows/repotting-workflow.md).

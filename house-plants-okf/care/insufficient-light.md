# Insufficient Light

Insufficient light slows growth and water use. A plant in weak light may stay wet longer after watering, stretch toward the window, lose variegation, or make small pale leaves.

## Diagnostic Use

* Stretching and long internodes point toward [leggy growth](/problems/leggy-growth.md).
* Yellowing in a persistently wet pot may combine weak light with [yellow leaves from overwatering](/problems/yellow-leaves-overwatering.md).
* Correction usually means moving closer to a suitable window or using [artificial lighting](/care/artificial-lighting.md).

# Relative Humidity

Relative humidity affects how quickly leaves lose water. Many tropical houseplants tolerate normal homes, but ferns and thin-leaved tropicals may suffer in heated winter air.

## Scope

This overview defines air moisture around foliage. Use focused humidity-stress and microclimate concepts when diagnosing leaf tips or pest pressure.

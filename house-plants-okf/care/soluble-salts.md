# Soluble Salts

Soluble salts accumulate when minerals from [fertilizer](/care/fertilizer.md) or water remain in the potting medium. White crust on soil or pot edges is a visible clue.

## Effects

Soluble salts can cause brown leaf tips, leaf drop, and root stress that mimics poor [watering](/care/watering.md).

Leaching requires a [drainage hole](/care/drainage-hole.md). Sensitive plants may show mineral or water-quality stress earlier than tougher plants.

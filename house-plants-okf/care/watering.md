# Watering

Watering is a decision based on plant type, pot size, medium, drainage, light, temperature, humidity, and current moisture. Calendar watering fails because plants use water differently as conditions change.

## Scope

This overview defines watering as a care domain. More focused concepts handle the graph relationships: moisture checking, dry-down targets, demand changes, excess water, and water deficit.

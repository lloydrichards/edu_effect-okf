# Light Intensity

Light intensity controls photosynthesis, growth rate, internode length, leaf size, flowering potential, and water use. Indoor light is usually described qualitatively as low, medium, high, bright indirect, or direct sun.

## Scope

This overview defines light as a care domain. More focused concepts handle placement, insufficient light, direct exposure, acclimation, and grow-light use.

* UGA Extension and Clemson HGIC both emphasize light as a primary indoor plant factor.

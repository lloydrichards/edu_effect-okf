# Light Acclimation

Light acclimation prevents damage when a plant moves from dim or greenhouse conditions into brighter exposure. It is especially important before direct sun touches foliage.

## Diagnostic Use

Abrupt exposure can cause [leaf scorch](/problems/leaf-scorch.md). Gradual movement lets new leaves adapt while older shade-grown leaves remain protected.

# Bright Indirect Light

Bright indirect light is strong diffuse light, often near an east window, several feet back from a south or west window, or behind a sheer curtain. It supports many tropical foliage plants without causing sunburn.

If growth stretches, the plant may need more [light intensity](/care/light-intensity.md) or [artificial lighting](/care/artificial-lighting.md). If leaves bleach or crisp after a move, check for [direct sun](/care/direct-sun.md) and [leaf scorch](/problems/leaf-scorch.md).

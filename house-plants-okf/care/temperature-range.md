# Temperature Range

Many houseplants grow best in comfortable human indoor temperatures, but tropical foliage plants can be injured by cold windows, drafts, or winter transport.

## Links

* Cold exposure can cause [chilling injury](/care/chilling-injury.md).
* Temperature affects [watering](/care/watering.md) because warm plants use and lose water faster.
* Placement near vents or drafty [window exposure](/care/window-exposure.md) can destabilize a [microclimate](/foundations/microclimate.md).

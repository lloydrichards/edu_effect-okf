# Dry-Down

Dry-down is the amount of drying a plant should experience before the next watering. It varies by plant type, season, light, pot size, and medium.

## Practical Meaning

Succulents usually need a stronger dry-down than ferns. A dry-down target is more useful than a calendar schedule because [watering demand](/care/watering-demand.md) changes over time.

# Underwatering

Underwatering occurs when the root zone dries beyond a plant's tolerance. It can be caused by infrequent watering, a pot that is too small, hydrophobic old potting mix, high heat, high light, or low humidity.

## Links

* Causes dry-soil [wilting](/problems/wilting.md).
* Can contribute to [brown leaf tips](/problems/brown-leaf-tips.md).
* Is especially risky for ferns and thin-leaved tropical plants.
* Is tolerated better by drought-adapted succulents than by thin-leaved ferns.

# Fertilizer

Fertilizer supplements nutrients but cannot compensate for poor [light intensity](/care/light-intensity.md), damaged roots, or incorrect [watering](/care/watering.md). Indoor plants usually need less fertilizer in winter or under low light.

## Risks

* Overuse creates [soluble salts](/care/soluble-salts.md).
* Salt stress can cause leaf-tip burn and leaf drop.
* Recently [repotted](/care/repotting.md) or stressed plants should not be pushed with heavy fertilizer.

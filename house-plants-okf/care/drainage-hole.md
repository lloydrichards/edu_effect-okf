# Drainage Hole

A drainage hole allows water to move through the potting medium and out of the container. It makes thorough watering and [soluble salts](/care/soluble-salts.md) leaching possible while reducing [overwatering](/care/overwatering.md) risk.

## Related Concepts

* [Watering](/care/watering.md) is safer when excess water can escape.
* Root rot risk rises in containers that trap water.
* Decorative cachepots can work if the inner nursery pot drains and excess water is emptied.
* [Repotting](/care/repotting.md) is a chance to correct poor container drainage.

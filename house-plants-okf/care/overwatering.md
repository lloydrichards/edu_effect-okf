# Overwatering

Overwatering is not only the amount of water poured into a pot; it is the duration of saturated, poorly aerated conditions. It is more likely when a plant has low light, a pot without a [drainage hole](/care/drainage-hole.md), an oversized container, or dense [growing medium](/care/growing-medium.md).

## Symptoms and Effects

Overwatering may show up as yellowing leaves, wet-soil wilting, leaf drop, or root decay. Remedies include improving drainage, letting the medium dry appropriately, repotting into a better mix, and adjusting [watering](/care/watering.md) frequency.

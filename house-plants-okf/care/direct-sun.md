# Direct Sun

Direct sun can be essential for high-light plants but harmful to foliage plants grown for filtered tropical understory conditions. Duration, season, glass heat, and prior [acclimation](/foundations/acclimation.md) determine whether direct sun helps or injures.

## Often Appropriate

* High-light succulents
* Some cacti
* Many cacti and arid succulents

## Common Risks

* [Leaf scorch](/problems/leaf-scorch.md) after abrupt exposure.
* Higher [watering](/care/watering.md) demand because high light and heat dry pots faster.
* Heat stress near south or west [window exposure](/care/window-exposure.md).

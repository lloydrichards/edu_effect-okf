# Artificial Lighting

Artificial lighting helps compensate for weak [window exposure](/care/window-exposure.md), short winter days, or plant shelves far from windows. Intensity, distance, spectrum, and photoperiod matter more than simply owning a grow light.

## Uses

* Remedy [leggy growth](/problems/leggy-growth.md) caused by insufficient light.
* Support flowering plants in dim homes.
* Keep shelf-grown foliage plants more compact.

More light usually increases [watering](/care/watering.md) demand and can justify modest [fertilizer](/care/fertilizer.md) during active growth.

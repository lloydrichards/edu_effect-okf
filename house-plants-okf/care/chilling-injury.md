# Chilling Injury

Chilling injury occurs when sensitive tropical plants are exposed to cold air, cold glass, winter transport, or drafts. Symptoms can include water-soaked patches, blackened tissue, yellowing leaves, and leaf drop.

Prevent it by checking [temperature range](/care/temperature-range.md), avoiding cold [window exposure](/care/window-exposure.md), and using good [acclimation](/foundations/acclimation.md) after purchase.

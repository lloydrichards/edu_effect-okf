# House Plants OKF

A medium-sized Open Knowledge Format bundle for exploring houseplant care, diagnostics, plant profiles, and graph relationships.

## Knowledge Areas

* [Foundations](./foundations/) - Core vocabulary for indoor plant care.
* [Care](./care/) - Environmental requirements and routine care concepts.
* [Plants](./plants/) - Representative houseplant profiles across difficulty levels.
* [Problems](./problems/) - Diagnostic symptoms and physiological disorders.
* [Pests](./pests/) - Common indoor pests and response strategies.
* [Propagation](./propagation/) - Methods for making new plants.
* [Workflows](./workflows/) - Practical care and troubleshooting procedures.
* [Safety](./safety/) - Pet toxicity and ingestion-response concepts.

## Useful Graph Starting Points

* [Houseplant](./foundations/houseplant.md) connects the whole domain.
* [Light Intensity](./care/light-intensity.md) is a major environmental hub.
* [Watering](./care/watering.md) links containers, potting mix, root rot, and diagnostics.
* [Diagnosis Workflow](./workflows/diagnosis-workflow.md) connects symptoms to likely causes and remedies.
* [Pet Toxicity](./safety/pet-toxicity.md) cuts across plant profiles.

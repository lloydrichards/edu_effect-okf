# Acclimation

Acclimation reduces shock when a plant moves from greenhouse, store, shipping box, outdoor patio, or another room into a new indoor environment.

## Common Triggers

* Moving from [bright indirect light](/care/bright-indirect-light.md) to [direct sun](/care/direct-sun.md).
* Moving from humid production conditions to dry indoor air.
* Exposure to cold drafts that can cause [chilling injury](/care/chilling-injury.md).
* Repotting into a new [growing medium](/care/growing-medium.md).

Poor acclimation can lead to leaf drop, scorch, or temporary yellowing.

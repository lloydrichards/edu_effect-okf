# Microclimate

A microclimate is the immediate environment around a plant. A bathroom shelf, a south window, a drafty sill, a plant cabinet, and a clustered group of pots can all create different microclimates within the same home.

## Factors

* [Window exposure](/care/window-exposure.md) changes light intensity and heat.
* [Relative humidity](/care/relative-humidity.md) changes transpiration and leaf-tip stress.
* [Temperature range](/care/temperature-range.md) changes growth rate and cold injury risk.
* Grouping plants can raise local humidity for humidity-sensitive species.

Microclimates explain why identical plants in the same home can need different [watering](/care/watering.md) schedules.

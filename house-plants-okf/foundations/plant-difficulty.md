# Plant Difficulty

Plant difficulty describes how much precision a plant requires in light, water, humidity, temperature, substrate, and pest monitoring. It is not a moral ranking; it is a planning tool for matching plants to homes and caretakers.

## Levels

* Beginner plants tolerate wider ranges or recover well.
* Intermediate plants need steadier care or have narrower tolerances.
* Advanced plants punish inconsistent humidity, minerals, or watering.

Difficulty often rises when a plant requires high [relative humidity](/care/relative-humidity.md), specialized [growing medium](/care/growing-medium.md), strong [light intensity](/care/light-intensity.md), or careful [watering](/care/watering.md).

# Repotting Workflow

1. Confirm the reason: root-bound growth, degraded [growing medium](/care/growing-medium.md), poor drainage, instability, or [root rot](/problems/root-rot.md).
2. Choose a pot with a [drainage hole](/care/drainage-hole.md), usually only modestly larger.
3. Match the medium to the plant: airy for epiphytes, fast-draining for succulents, moisture-retentive but aerated for ferns.
4. Remove dead roots if present.
5. Water appropriately and avoid heavy [fertilizer](/care/fertilizer.md) immediately after stress.
6. Monitor for [wilting](/problems/wilting.md), [leaf drop](/problems/leaf-drop.md), and pests.

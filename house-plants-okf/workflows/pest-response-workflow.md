# Pest Response Workflow

1. Use [quarantine](/pests/quarantine.md) immediately.
2. Inspect leaf undersides, stems, crowns, axils, and soil surface.
3. Identify the pest: [spider mites](/pests/spider-mites.md), [mealybugs](/pests/mealybugs.md), [scale insects](/pests/scale-insects.md), or [fungus gnats](/pests/fungus-gnats.md).
4. Remove visible pests mechanically when possible.
5. Correct cultural contributors such as dry [relative humidity](/care/relative-humidity.md) or wet [growing medium](/care/growing-medium.md).
6. Apply appropriate labeled treatment and repeat inspection.

This is the action version of [integrated pest management](/pests/integrated-pest-management.md).

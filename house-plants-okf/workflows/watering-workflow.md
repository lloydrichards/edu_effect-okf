# Watering Workflow

1. Identify the plant's moisture preference from its profile.
2. Use [soil moisture check](/care/soil-moisture-check.md) before watering.
3. Apply the right [dry-down](/care/dry-down.md) target.
4. Adjust for [watering demand](/care/watering-demand.md).
5. If symptoms appear, compare excess water with water deficit.

This workflow should route to focused moisture concepts rather than becoming a broad graph hub.

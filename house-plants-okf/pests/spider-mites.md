# Spider Mites

Spider mites feed on leaves and can create fine webbing. They often become worse in warm, dry conditions.

## Graph Links

* Dry [relative humidity](/care/relative-humidity.md) favors outbreaks.
* Symptoms may include stippling, dull leaves, [yellowing leaves](/problems/yellowing-leaves.md), and webbing.
* Sensitive or attractive hosts include thin-leaved tropicals.
* Response starts with [quarantine](/pests/quarantine.md) and [integrated pest management](/pests/integrated-pest-management.md).

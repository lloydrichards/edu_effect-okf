# Pests

Common indoor pests and response concepts.

* [Integrated Pest Management](./integrated-pest-management.md) - Inspection, isolation, cultural correction, and treatment.
* [Quarantine](./quarantine.md) - Separating new or infested plants.
* [Spider Mites](./spider-mites.md) - Dry-air pest causing stippling and webbing.
* [Mealybugs](./mealybugs.md) - Cottony sap-feeding insects.
* [Scale Insects](./scale-insects.md) - Armored or soft sap-feeding pests.
* [Fungus Gnats](./fungus-gnats.md) - Small flies associated with wet potting media.

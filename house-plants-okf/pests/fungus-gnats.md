# Fungus Gnats

Fungus gnats are small flies often noticed around damp potting media. Adults are annoying; larvae can affect tender roots in heavy infestations.

## Links

* Often associated with [overwatering](/care/overwatering.md) or constantly wet [growing medium](/care/growing-medium.md).
* Improve [watering](/care/watering.md) habits and surface drying where plant-safe.
* Use [integrated pest management](/pests/integrated-pest-management.md) and [quarantine](/pests/quarantine.md) if needed.

# Quarantine

Quarantine protects a plant collection by keeping a new or suspect plant physically separated while pests and symptoms reveal themselves.

## When to Use

* After buying a plant in [choosing-and-placing-a-plant](/workflows/choosing-and-placing-a-plant.md).
* When [mealybugs](/pests/mealybugs.md), [scale insects](/pests/scale-insects.md), [spider mites](/pests/spider-mites.md), or [fungus gnats](/pests/fungus-gnats.md) are detected.
* During [pest response workflow](/workflows/pest-response-workflow.md).

# Mealybugs

Mealybugs are common houseplant pests that feed on sap and produce waxy white material. They spread through plant contact, new purchases, and hidden infestations.

## Links

* Use [quarantine](/pests/quarantine.md) for new or infested plants.
* They can cause [yellowing leaves](/problems/yellowing-leaves.md), sticky residue, and decline.
* Large aroids should be inspected in leaf axils and nodes.
* Manage through [integrated pest management](/pests/integrated-pest-management.md).

# Scale Insects

Scale insects feed on sap and may look like brown, tan, or waxy bumps. Soft scale can produce honeydew and sticky leaves.

## Links

* Causes plant stress, [yellowing leaves](/problems/yellowing-leaves.md), and [leaf drop](/problems/leaf-drop.md).
* Commonly found on stems, undersides, and older woody indoor plants.
* Response requires [quarantine](/pests/quarantine.md) and repeated [integrated pest management](/pests/integrated-pest-management.md).

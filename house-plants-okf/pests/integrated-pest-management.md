# Integrated Pest Management

Integrated pest management, or IPM, reduces pest problems without treating every symptom as a chemical problem.

## Steps

* Inspect new plants and use [quarantine](/pests/quarantine.md).
* Identify the pest: [spider mites](/pests/spider-mites.md), [mealybugs](/pests/mealybugs.md), [scale insects](/pests/scale-insects.md), or [fungus gnats](/pests/fungus-gnats.md).
* Correct stressors such as low [relative humidity](/care/relative-humidity.md), poor [watering](/care/watering.md), or dusty leaves.
* Remove visible pests and use appropriate labeled treatments.

See [pest response workflow](/workflows/pest-response-workflow.md).

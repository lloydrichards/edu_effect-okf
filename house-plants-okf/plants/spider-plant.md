# Spider Plant

Spider plant (*Chlorophytum comosum*) is a resilient plant that produces plantlets on stolons and is useful for propagation teaching.

## Care Graph

* Light: medium to bright indirect light.
* Water: moderate watering, avoiding extremes.
* Symptom: brown tips can occur with salts, low humidity, or water-quality issues.
* Propagation: [division](/propagation/division.md) and plantlet rooting.
* Safety: often selected when pet toxicity is a concern; verify current ASPCA listing.

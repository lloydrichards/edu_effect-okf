# Dracaena

Dracaena includes common indoor plants such as corn plant. Many tolerate lower light but are vulnerable to root problems and leaf-tip burn.

## Care Graph

* Light: low to medium light.
* Water: let the medium partly dry; avoid [overwatering](/care/overwatering.md).
* Sensitivity: [soluble salts](/care/soluble-salts.md) and water quality can cause [brown leaf tips](/problems/brown-leaf-tips.md).
* Temperature: avoid cold drafts.
* Safety: ASPCA lists dracaena as toxic to cats and dogs.

# Peace Lily

Peace lily (*Spathiphyllum* spp.) is valued for glossy leaves and white spathes. It is tolerant of lower light but flowers better with adequate light.

## Care Graph

* Light: low to medium light.
* Water: responds visibly to underwatering with wilting, but can suffer root rot if kept soggy.
* Humidity: appreciates moderate relative humidity.
* Propagation: [division](/propagation/division.md).
* Safety: toxic to pets due to calcium oxalates.

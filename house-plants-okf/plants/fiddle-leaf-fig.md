# Fiddle-Leaf Fig

Fiddle-leaf fig (*Ficus lyrata*) is prized for large leaves but often reacts to unstable light, drafts, or watering changes.

## Care Graph

* Light: bright indirect light to high filtered light.
* Stability: needs careful [acclimation](/foundations/acclimation.md).
* Water: avoid both underwatering and [overwatering](/care/overwatering.md).
* Symptoms: leaf drop, brown tips, and scorch.
* Pest: monitor with [integrated pest management](/pests/integrated-pest-management.md).

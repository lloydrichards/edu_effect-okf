# Maidenhair Fern

Maidenhair fern (*Adiantum* spp.) is delicate and intolerant of drying, making it a useful advanced-care concept.

## Care Graph

* Light: medium to bright indirect light.
* Humidity: high relative humidity, often requiring a favorable [microclimate](/foundations/microclimate.md).
* Water: consistent moisture; avoid drying cycles.
* Symptoms: rapid wilting, frond browning, and brown tips.
* Propagation: [division](/propagation/division.md).

# Snake Plant

Snake plant (*Dracaena trifasciata*, formerly *Sansevieria trifasciata*) is tolerant of neglect and low light, but it declines quickly in saturated media.

## Care Graph

* Light: low to high light.
* Water: strong dry-down between watering.
* Risk: [overwatering](/care/overwatering.md) and root rot.
* Propagation: division or [leaf cuttings](/propagation/leaf-cuttings.md).
* Safety: ASPCA lists snake plant as toxic to cats and dogs.

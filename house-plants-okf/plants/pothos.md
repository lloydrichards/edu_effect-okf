# Pothos

Pothos (*Epipremnum aureum*) is a forgiving trailing foliage plant useful for beginner care and propagation demonstrations.

## Care Graph

* Difficulty: beginner.
* Light: low to bright indirect light; too little light can cause [leggy growth](/problems/leggy-growth.md).
* Water: let the mix partly dry between watering; avoid [overwatering](/care/overwatering.md).
* Propagation: [stem cuttings](/propagation/stem-cuttings.md).
* Safety: toxic to many pets due to insoluble calcium oxalates in many aroids.

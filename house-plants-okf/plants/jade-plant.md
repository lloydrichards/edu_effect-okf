# Jade Plant

Jade plant (*Crassula ovata*) stores water in thick leaves and stems. It is easy when light is strong and the root zone dries appropriately.

## Care Graph

* Light: high light and some direct sun after acclimation.
* Water: avoid frequent watering; use fast-draining potting mix.
* Problem: [overwatering](/care/overwatering.md) causes root rot.
* Propagation: [stem cuttings](/propagation/stem-cuttings.md) or [leaf cuttings](/propagation/leaf-cuttings.md).
* Safety: ASPCA lists jade as toxic to cats and dogs.

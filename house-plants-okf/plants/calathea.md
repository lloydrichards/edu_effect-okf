# Calathea

Calathea and related *Goeppertia* species are grown for patterned foliage. They are graph-rich because many care factors converge on leaf-edge symptoms.

## Care Graph

* Light: bright indirect light, avoiding direct sun.
* Humidity: higher relative humidity.
* Water: consistent moisture, not soggy or bone dry.
* Sensitivity: [soluble salts](/care/soluble-salts.md) and water-quality issues can cause [brown leaf tips](/problems/brown-leaf-tips.md).
* Pest: dry conditions increase mite risk.

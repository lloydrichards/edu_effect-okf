# Aloe Vera

Aloe vera is a common succulent grown for its rosette form. Indoors it needs bright conditions and excellent drainage.

## Care Graph

* Light: high light, often near direct sun.
* Water: deep but infrequent watering.
* Medium: fast-draining mix and a draining container.
* Problem: wet conditions cause root rot.
* Safety: ASPCA lists aloe as toxic to cats and dogs.

# Monstera

Monstera (*Monstera deliciosa*) is a climbing tropical aroid with large fenestrated leaves when mature and well lit.

## Care Graph

* Light: bright indirect light; insufficient light causes leggy growth.
* Water: moderate watering in a chunky mix.
* Diagnostic issue: [monstera yellow leaves](/problems/monstera-yellow-leaves.md).
* Maintenance: repotting, pruning, and support may be needed.
* Pest: inspect for sap-feeding pests.
* Safety: toxic aroid; verify pet-safety guidance.

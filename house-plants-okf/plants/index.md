# Plants

Representative plant profiles for graph exploration.

* [Pothos](./pothos.md) - Tolerant trailing aroid.
* [Snake Plant](./snake-plant.md) - Drought-tolerant upright foliage plant.
* [Spider Plant](./spider-plant.md) - Easy plantlet-producing foliage plant.
* [Peace Lily](./peace-lily.md) - Low-to-medium light flowering foliage plant.
* [Jade Plant](./jade-plant.md) - High-light succulent shrub.
* [Aloe Vera](./aloe-vera.md) - High-light succulent with rot sensitivity.
* [Monstera](./monstera.md) - Large climbing aroid.
* [Fiddle-Leaf Fig](./fiddle-leaf-fig.md) - Popular but change-sensitive indoor tree.
* [Boston Fern](./boston-fern.md) - Humidity-loving fern.
* [Calathea](./calathea.md) - Humidity and water-quality sensitive foliage plant.
* [Dracaena](./dracaena.md) - Common upright foliage plant with mineral sensitivity.
* [Orchid](./orchid.md) - Epiphytic flowering houseplant profile.
* [Maidenhair Fern](./maidenhair-fern.md) - Advanced fern requiring steady moisture.

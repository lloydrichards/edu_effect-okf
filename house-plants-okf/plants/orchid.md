# Orchid

Many houseplant orchids, especially *Phalaenopsis*, are epiphytes adapted to airy root zones rather than dense potting soil.

## Care Graph

* Light: bright indirect light, sometimes improved with supplemental lighting.
* Medium: airy bark-based medium.
* Water: soak and drain thoroughly; avoid standing water and root rot.
* Temperature: comfortable indoor temperatures, with species-specific needs.
* Safety: many common orchids are pet-safer, but verify the species.

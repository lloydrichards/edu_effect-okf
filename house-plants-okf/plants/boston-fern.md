# Boston Fern

Boston fern (*Nephrolepis exaltata*) is a classic hanging fern with fine fronds and higher moisture needs.

## Care Graph

* Light: medium to bright indirect light.
* Humidity: benefits from higher relative humidity.
* Water: keep evenly moist without overwatering.
* Symptoms: brown tips, frond shedding, or wilting when too dry.
* Propagation: [division](/propagation/division.md).
